### 1. Information We Collect

When you use the Shioru bot, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Guild Information (Discord Server)**:

  - Guild ID, name, icon, and verified status
  - Creation date and date the bot joined
  - Number of members
  - Language and region settings

- **User Information**:

  - Discord user ID
  - Level and experience data (global and per-guild)

- **Message Content** (processed in real-time, not stored permanently):

  - Messages directed at the bot (via @mention or reply) are read to provide chat responses
  - Up to 10 recent messages in a channel may be retrieved temporarily as conversation context for AI responses
  - Message content is **not stored** in our database

- **Usage Information**:

  - Number of times each command was used
  - Aggregate statistics (total commands, guilds, users, completed tasks)

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the features of the bot
- Track and analyze usage patterns to improve the user experience
- Respond to requests and support users
- Provide AI-powered chat responses
- Investigate and prevent violations of the Terms of Use

---

### 3. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Features

Shioru uses AI-powered features for chat responses. When you interact with the bot's chat system:

- Your message and up to 10 recent messages in the channel are sent to **OpenAI** (via their API) to generate a response
- The AI model used is **GPT-4o-mini**
- Messages are sent in real-time and are **not stored by Shioru** after the response is generated
- OpenAI may process this data according to their own [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- Guild administrators can configure the chat system or disable AI responses via the dashboard

### 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Guild settings (language, DJ, notifications) | Until the bot leaves the guild or deletion is requested |
| User leveling data (EXP, level) | Until the user requests deletion |
| Message content | **Not stored** - processed in real-time only |
| AI API requests | Retained by OpenAI per their policy |
| Aggregate statistics | Indefinite (anonymized, non-personal) |

### 6. Data Security

We use appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of information. Data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database with access controls.

---

### 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
- **Right to Delete**: Use the `/forget` command in Discord or contact us to request deletion of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Contact us to correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Object**: Disable specific features (such as AI chat) via guild settings or the dashboard

**Timeline**: Data deletion requests are processed within **30 days**.

If you believe your rights have been violated, you may contact your local data protection authority.

---

### 8. Children's Privacy
